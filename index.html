BIOGRAPHY:

My name is Francisco Cardoso, I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. All my life I've only known the lone star state and have studied here my entire life K-12. I attended college at Texas Tech University for a semester and a half before covid shutdown the entire world. After the lockdown I lost interest in my studies, pre-engineering at the time, and decided to focus on my job and saving money. At the time I was working as a server and for what it was it was great money. Since the start of 2022 I felt like I needed to finish my education and I began researching how to do so. I gained an interest in software engineering because I love the thrill of solving problems through functionality. Towards the end of the year I looked into it and found General Assembly. Thanks to my 2 years of hard work I payed for their software engineering program and haven't looked back. I am currently on a self-improvement journey for the time lost and I am ready to take on any challenge that lies ahead.
